Our Mission

At freedomap we believe that your right to protest should be protected and encouraged which is why we why we have created freedomap. freedomap is an accessible web application that allows users to document and view protest opportunities in their area and around the world. With freedomap, you can join protests and exercise your right to protest, anywhere, any time.
